Brand Identity

Your domain is your digital address. It should be memorable and consistent with the "JoyShree" name to reinforce your brand. While not product-specific, its positive meaning of "Joy" is a strong asset.

Audience & Reach

The domain extension (like .in or .com) signals who you're for. A `.in` builds trust in India, while `.com` is for a global audience. E-commerce extensions like `.shop` clearly state your purpose.

SEO & Discoverability

While the domain itself is just one part of SEO, owning multiple relevant domains and redirecting them to your main site helps consolidate traffic and authority, making you easier to find on search engines.
